About

Gospel Singer & Songwriter

Oneil Jackson is a Jamaican Gospel Singer/Songwriter who has been singing for over 15 years. His projects mainly focuses on the contemporary and revival style of music.

    Given Name: Oneil Jackson
    Birthday: February 01, 1992
    Birthplace: Point Hill, St Catherine
    Current City: Portmore, St Catherine
    Nationality: Jamaican
    Phone: Request Phone Number
    Email: jacksononeil955@gmail.com
    Label: Distrokid

Oneil Jackson has always had a love for God. This dates back to when he was a student at the Point Hill Leased Primary and Junior High School where he realized that he had a desire for God and wanted to live a holy life. On December 31, 2007, he decided to take his watery baptism and he started to follow Jesus by doing his work full time in ministry. Jackson's music ministry started on Princess Street, Darling Street, Tivoli and Coronation Market, Downtown Kingston. These are some of the places he would go and sing at street meetings. Today, Oneil Jackson better known as Minister Howdy is an accomplished Gospel Recording Artiste. Feel free to look around and book him for any event you have using the contact information displayed or visit the platforms to listen his music.
